#GivingBackToCommunity

A Digital Initiative of Uplers for NotForProfits

OUR PARTNERS



OUR INITIATIVE


  • Here at Uplers, we appreciate the hard work that gets done in the not-for-profit (NFP) sector with limited resources.

  • Many hard-working NFPs would benefit from greater online exposure, but few can afford the expense of working with an agency like us.

  • We believe it’s important to use our skills for more than just commercial gain. So now, with help from Google, we’ve found a unique way to donate our search marketing expertise to NFPs.

  • Google Ad Grants, it’s a generous initiative whereby approved NFPs receive up to $10,000 per month via in-kind AdWordsTM advertising.

  • Until now, there was a loophole with the program: Google provides no management support for your online ad campaigns. For those NFPs with limited digital know-how, a poorly managed campaign is a huge opportunity wasted.

  • This is where Uplers comes in under the #GivingBackToCommunity initiative, we donate our digital expertise to help your NFP to get more bang for your buck from a Google Ad Grant at zero cost. It’s our way of giving back.

HOW WE MAKE A DIFFERENCE







HOW WE WORK

Google provides not-for-profits a grant of $10,000 per month, in-kind.

We manage it completely free.

Not-for-profits get visibility and reach on Google.

We continuously update our strategies

Not-for-profits gain engagement, online coverage & volunteers to help.
