Business Referral Program


Teamwork makes the Dreamwork!


Uplers is one of the largest, globally recognized outsourcing agencies in the industry, empowering businesses across the globe to build a better digital ecosystem. With over 8 years of experience, a team of over 650 marketers and developers, and 7000+ global clients, we have helped various companies achieve their goals, scale their operations, and provide their business with an edge over the others in the market.

We take pride in the fact that many of our clients have found their way to us through recommendations from our existing clients.

Refer & Earn


The right connections foster the right partnerships. Through the medium of this business referral policy, we are on the lookout for the right recommendations with recurring needs. Our business referral program is a relationship-driven revenue channel aimed at building strong relationships based on trust, values, and a growth mindset.

Our business referral program works in the following ways:

One-off project

A one-off project means anything that has a completion date, majorly in website development, email templates, design projects, and ad-hoc tasks. We pay a 20% kick-back straight once the invoices are paid by the referral. Such projects can be divided into 2 or 3 equal installments over a period of 1 to 6 months.

Recurring Business

A recurring business is anything that is continuous in nature, examples are SEO/SEM campaigns and Offshore Remote Teams model, which has a ﬁxed monthly payment commitment. Such commitments come with a minimum of 3 months contract and remain ongoing until speciﬁcally canceled. In such cases, a 10% kick-back would be paid on a monthly basis, once the invoices are paid by the referral.

What’s in it for you?


Being a part of our business referral policy has several beneﬁts. The policy leads to a clear win-win outcome for everyone.





Loyalties with our clients are what we value the most. By way of this referral policy, we as an organization will always be a loyal stakeholder for your business.



When you bring someone in contact with our organization, you do so out of trust and conﬁdence in us. We believe it is our duty to honor that trust and do our best to fulfill our partner's needs.



We are known for our quality, consistency, and our principle of always adhering to timelines. Anyone you recommend to our business will always experience the same unmatched service from us.



Referring our organization to someone in your network will give them access to our army of 650+ experts that are masters at their domains and bring in years of valuable experience to uplift businesses.

Ready to Refer? Get in Touch


You can get in touch with us by simply contacting your Account Manager, Customer Success Manager, or Sales Representative.

Alternatively, share your referral details or introduce us at hello@uplers.com.

Terms & Conditions


There are some points of agreement that require prior consideration before enrolling in the business referral program.


  • If you sell a one-off project, you get 20% of the project value as a kick-back.

  • If you help us close the client into recurring engagement, you will get 10% of the contract value that will be paid monthly on the receipt of realization for the ﬁrst 12 months.

  • If the payment from the referred client gets canceled for any reason, it will result in the cancellation of the referral also.

  • An invoice would be required to be generated every month from your end.

FAQs



When will I get paid?

Once we receive the payment from the referee, we will process your payment within 15 working days.

What if the referee went away without payment?

In such cases, there would not be any payment to the referrer.

What if the referee gave another project? Will I be paid?

The current referral policy is limited to the ﬁrst project only.




How do I get paid?

We request you to raise an invoice, which would be paid within 15 working days.

I know a potential client. How do I refer?

You can share their details with us, one of our sales representatives can directly get in touch with them. We are open to not sharing the details of the referee if needed.

Who do I share the referral with?

If you are a present client, you can introduce your referral with your Client Success Manager, Account Manager, or Sales Representative and they shall take it ahead from there. Alternatively, you can introduce at hello@uplers.com.
