Description

The workshop is designed to help startup-preneurs to build a clear and well thought through system, understanding & strategy around the basic legal and financial elements of running any business.

Entrepreneurs who are about to start a new business/start-up or looking to expand their existing business need to have a basic understanding of legal and financial tools like, contracts, agreements, incorporation, pricing, revenue models, and business planning, to help them achieve excellence in their endeavor to have a successful business. This course aims to help them by taking only a fraction of their time in achieving this target.
