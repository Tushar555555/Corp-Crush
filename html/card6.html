About Course

Ideas are Dime a Dazon, What makes your Idea worth is it's being a Business Opportunity. Learn how to Identify, Assess, and Validated your idea with us.


Description

By the end of this module, you will be able to validate your idea. And will answer the following questions for your idea.

Technical & Market Feasibility (Does the need exist?)

a. What pain point are you solving for?

b. Who are you solving this problem for?

c. How are you going to solve the problem?

d. Is anyone else doing it? How are customers currently solving their problems?

e. Can you do it differently from them

Financial Feasibility (Can you monetize the idea?)

a. Would your customers pay for your solution to a particular problem?

b. How big is the market? How many customers would potentially pay for your solution?
